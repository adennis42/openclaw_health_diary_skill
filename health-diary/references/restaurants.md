# Restaurant Reference

Regional and chain restaurants with known nutrition data sources.
Always attempt a live web lookup first — use this as a fallback.

## How to Look Up a Restaurant

Priority order:
1. `{restaurant}.com/nutrition` or `{restaurant}.com/menu`
2. `calorieking.com` search
3. `nutritionix.com` search
4. Google: `"{restaurant}" "{item}" calories`

## Northeast US Regional Chains

### Friendly's (New England / Mid-Atlantic / Long Island)
- Classic diner chain known for ice cream + full menu
- Nutrition page: https://www.friendlys.com/menu
- Common items (estimates if lookup fails):

  - Burger (classic): ~700–800 cal
